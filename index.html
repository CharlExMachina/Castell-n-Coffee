Who we are

Cafetalera Castellón S.A. is a company dedicated to the production of coffee. We are positioned through out all of the value chain, offering high standard exportation quality that comes from the harvest, elaboration of the final product, qualified staff, control of processing systems and great service offered to our clients.

Mission

We are a coffee processing plant that integrates and practices its values, it operates effectively in order to satisfy our clients, offering a Nicaraguan coffee with high quality and competitiveness.

  • Directly from the coffee farms of Nicaragua
  • Preserving the purity of the product
  • Offering excellent quality in every grain

Our Services

CASTELLÓN COFFEE S.A. provide the best services for established companies or those that look for venture into the market with new trends and innovations that give their customers the quality and excellence they desire.

We offer raw material processing throughout the value chain to deliver roasted and ground quality coffee, we have a highly qualified team, high-tech system and certified products that provide greater consumer confidence.
